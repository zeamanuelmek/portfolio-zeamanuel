## The Problem

Ethiopian startups need professional web presence fast. Building from scratch takes **3+ weeks per site**.
Most generic templates don't reflect Ethiopian culture or business patterns.

## The Solution

SemEnaWerk is a **white-label master template** with:

- **23 themed atoms**: buttons, cards, inputs, all brand-ready
- **Multi-tenant theming**: swap brand colors in 5 minutes
- **Ethiopian design language**: gold accents, habesha patterns, warm earth tones
- **Production stack**: Next.js 16 + Mantine 7 + TypeScript
